A story of beautiful girl in New Zealand

A lovely girl,

With the wilderness view in New Zealand.

She lives in a cafe with an ancient piano,

And the white flower that was grown in her backyard.

She lift up her head,

And watch the beautiful view in New Zealand everyday.

When she is lonely,

She will go to talk with the verdant grass on the field,

And watch the transparent sea to calm down.

This is the story of a beautiful Girl that is in New Zealand.

THE END
